Frequently Asked Questions

What is PopCorn really?

PopCorn is a web application that presents itself as an online streaming service for movies. However, in its beginnings, PopCorn does not allow direct playback of its audiovisual content. Instead, its goal is to provide an intuitive interface for presenting, organizing, and filtering information regarding cinematographic content in an effective and satisfactory manner for users.

It is really a free platform?

Initially, PopCorn is presented as a completely free service accessible to everyone. However, due to high demand and the presence of numerous competitors in the market, the possibility of implementing a monthly subscription system will be considered.

Who are the potencial users of PopCorn?

PopCorn is an ideal application for users of any level of technological skill, age, culture, or gender. Additionally, having a fully adaptable interface, it can be satisfactorily represented on any device and size, so it does not depend on the type of device the user is using.
